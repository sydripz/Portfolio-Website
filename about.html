My Story

Believe it or not, skateboarding was what got me started in the design world. It was the first time I was immersed in an artistic community, an immediately knew I wanted my career to be have some sort of design heavy foundation. Fast forward about a year and I began studying UX design. I fell in love with the concept of user centered designs, and have now designed a health & fitness app from conception to development handoff.

My immersion in the UX world led to a desire to understand how the coding & development process works. This led me to gaining proficiency in HTML, CSS and JavaScript. I am very interested in work that pertains to the health & wellness space. I would be eager to explore the opportunity to work for a sports organization or athletic wear company. As someone who loves to expand their knowledge and skillset, a role that allows me to grow professionally is highly important.
